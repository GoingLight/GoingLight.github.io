Breaking the Silence website by Going Light Corporation


This is Breaking the Silence website, a platform for audience to seek information about several mental health issues.
For instance, depression, anxiety, stress, eating disorder, schizophrenia and Alzheimerâ€™s disease. 
Besides, this website intended to create awareness among people to have better knowledge and understanding on mental health issue and being helpful for those who in need.
The name of the website per se gives hope whose suffered that there are still a light for them to cure
and we trying to reach them to give a hand by many approaches. 


---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Usage Notes

-This website provides information along with pictures to readers.
-This website also allows people to test themselves to check their health conditions.
-People can book appointment by filling form to meet the specialists in order to get help.
-Besides, people can also track the availability of specialists based on certain location by fill in the places.
-The website supports multiple web and mobile browser.
-Users are recommended to use web browser for better sight and reading.
