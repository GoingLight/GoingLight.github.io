Why do you need this?

Psychotherapy can provide help with a range of problems, from depression and low self-esteem to addiction and family disputes. Anyone who is feeling overwhelmed by their problems and unable to cope may be able to benefit from psychotherapy.

He has 12 years of experience dealing with child, adolescence (e.g. conduct and behavioral problems, ADHD) and adult mental health problems (e.g. trauma and stress, substance use, depression, anxiety, grief, and anger). Maria Teresa Aguilar Icasiano, MD, FPPA, F.P.C.Psych.
Senior Psychiatrist | Expert Trainer

Teresa is chair of the Psychiatry and Behavior Medicine Section at the Philippine Heart Center. She is a psychiatrist specializing in Consultation Liaison Psychiatry, with extensive experience in treating cardiac patients with psychological problems. Fervent and enthusiastic in teaching Psychiatry not only in medical schools but to other specialties as well. Tess is also a member of the American Psychosomatic Society, and the American Psychiatric Association.
