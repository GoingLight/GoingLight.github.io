Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia is a chronic and severe mental disorder that affects how a person thinks, feels, and behaves. People with schizophrenia may seem like they have lost touch with reality. Although schizophrenia is not as common as other mental disorders, the symptoms can be very disabling. schizophrenia treatments focus on eliminating the symptoms of the disease. Treatments include: (1)Antipsychotics (2)Psychosocial Treatments (3)Coordinated specialty care (CSC).
